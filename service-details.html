Full-Stack Infrastructure & Automation Services

I bridge the gap between traditional network engineering and modern cloud-native orchestration. My goal is to build resilient, secure, and automated systems that allow your business to scale without technical debt.

1. Hybrid Cloud & Kubernetes Orchestration

Move beyond simple VMs. I design and manage containerized environments that live where you need them—on-prem or in the cloud.

  • K8s Cluster Design: Production-grade deployment of upstream Kubernetes or managed services (EKS/AKS/GKE).
  • Legacy to Container Migration: Transitioning traditional "monolithic" applications into microservices.
  • Hybrid Integration: Ensuring your on-prem database talks seamlessly to your cloud-based K8s pods.

2. Next-Gen Security (Fortigate Specialist)

Infrastructure is only as good as its perimeter. I specialize in Fortinet-driven security architectures.

  • SD-WAN Implementation: Optimizing branch connectivity and reducing MPLS costs using FortiGate's advanced routing.
  • Secure Remote Access: Setting up ZTNA (Zero Trust Network Access) and high-performance VPNs.
  • Micro-segmentation: Protecting your internal network from lateral movement using VDOMs and internal firewalls.

3. Workflow Automation with n8n

Stop doing manual IT tasks. I build "Low-Code" brains that connect your infrastructure tools together.

  • Event-Driven Response: Automatically trigger Fortigate policy changes or K8s scaling based on external alerts.
  • Custom Integrations: Connecting your monitoring tools (Zabbix/Prometheus) to messaging apps (Slack/Teams) via n8n.
  • Self-Healing Infrastructure: Designing workflows that detect a failure and execute a recovery script automatically.

4. Modernized Network Engineering

Solid "Traditional" networking is the foundation of the cloud. I handle the heavy lifting of core infrastructure.

  • Core Routing & Switching: Expert configuration of BGP, OSPF, and VLAN architectures.
  • Wireless Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade WiFi deployments with seamless roaming.
  • Infrastructure as Code (IaC): Managing your traditional network hardware using Terraform or Ansible for consistency and speed.

5. Security & Automation Audit

Not sure where the bottleneck is? I provide a deep-dive analysis of your current stack.

  • Vulnerability Assessment: Checking your Fortigate and K8s configurations against industry benchmarks.
  • Automation ROI: Identifying manual processes in your IT team that can be replaced by n8n workflows.
  • Performance Tuning: Eliminating latency between your hybrid cloud nodes.
